## My Understanding (My Own Words)
- Security breaches today are very common because cybercriminals are
  always finding new vulnerabilities to infiltrate systems.
- A well-known hotel chain that operates worldwide reported a massive
  data breach, exposing the personal information of over three million
  guests to hackers.
- A popular online training platform admitted leaving the personal data
  of millions of students exposed — so choose your platform carefully.
- Once attackers obtain your personal information from breached sites,
  they can easily target you using phishing attack.
- Weaknesses in a platform's security practices are one of hackers'
  favorite targets (e.g. new platforms, unregulated or unofficial ones).
- Since cyberattacks are increasingly common, it is better to stay
  cyber-threat aware so you can protect yourself.
